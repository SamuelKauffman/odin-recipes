Salmon Cobb Salad

Salmon cobb salad

Salmon cobb salad is a very good choice for eating healthy and cheap. This very simple dish has a salty, refreshing, and light feel to it.

Ingredients

Dressing:

  • buttermilk ranch
  • Mayonnaise
  • Minced shallot
  • chopped fresh dill
  • lemon juice
  • salt

Salad:

  • head Bibb lettuce
  • cooked bacon
  • avocado
  • hard-boiled eggs
  • package smoked salmon
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk buttermilk, mayonnaise, shallot, dill, lemon juice, and salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth; refrigerate.
  2. Mix lettuce, bacon, avocado, eggs, and salmon together in a bowl. Add dressing and toss until coated; season with black pepper.
